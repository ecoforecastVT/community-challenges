# community-challenges
CEF-sponsored forecast challenges for the VT community and beyond

The Center for Ecosystem Forecasting (CEF) at Virginia Tech aims to enrich and elevate the enterprise of forecasting with fun challenges. 

The first challenge was issued in early February 2026, with the instructions, "To elevate the enterprise of modeling/forecasting at Virginia Tech (and have some low-stakes winter fun), the Center for Ecosystem Forecasting is hosting the Winter 2026 Prediction Challenge for its members and staff. To participate, make a prediction for the total number of gold, silver, and bronze medals you think the United States will receive at the upcoming 2026 Winter Olympics and submit your choices in an attached google form. The winner will be determined by minimizing summed absolute bias across the three medal predictions. If there are multiple winning predictions, we will randomly choose one of them as the winner, who will receive CEF swag as a prize."
